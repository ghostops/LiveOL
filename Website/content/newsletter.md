# Newsletter

Stay informed about the latest LiveOL features, updates, and orienteering insights.

## What You'll Get

Our newsletter includes:

- **Feature Announcements**: Be the first to know about new LiveOL capabilities
- **Competition Highlights**: Analysis and insights from major orienteering events
- **Technical Updates**: Behind-the-scenes look at how we improve live tracking
- **Community Stories**: Athlete interviews and user spotlights

## Frequency

We send newsletters monthly, with special editions for major competitions and updates.

## Subscribe

To subscribe to our newsletter, please send an email to **newsletter@orienteeringliveresults.com** with "Subscribe" in the subject line.

We respect your privacy and will never share your email address with third parties. You can unsubscribe at any time.
