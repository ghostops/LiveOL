# About LiveOL

LiveOL is the leading mobile application for real-time orienteering results and competition tracking. Built for athletes, coaches, spectators, and fans, LiveOL brings the excitement of orienteering competitions to your fingertips.

## Our Mission

We believe orienteering competitions should be accessible, engaging, and easy to follow in real-time. LiveOL bridges the gap between the forest and your device, delivering live results with sub-second precision and an intuitive, mobile-first interface.

## What We Offer

### For Athletes

- **Track your performance** across competitions
- **Analyze splits** and compare with competitors
- **Follow your club mates** and competitors in real-time
- **Access historical results** and personal statistics

### For Spectators

- **Live tracking** of runners during competitions
- **Real-time updates** with no delay
- **Multi-language support** for international events
- **Clean, mobile-optimized** interface

### For the Community

- **Comprehensive coverage** of orienteering events worldwide
- **Open platform** supporting multiple data sources
- **Active development** with regular feature updates
- **Community-driven** improvements and feedback

## Technical Excellence

LiveOL is engineered for precision and performance:

- **Sub-second synchronization** - Get results as they happen
- **Cross-platform** - Native iOS and Android apps
- **Reliable infrastructure** - 99.9% uptime during major championships
- **Scalable architecture** - Handles thousands of concurrent users

## Data Sources

We integrate with multiple data providers to bring you comprehensive coverage:

- **Liveresultat** (Sweden) - Real-time results from Swedish competitions
- **Eventor API** - Event information and entry lists
- **International federations** - Coverage of major championships
- **Direct integration** - Working with event organizers for optimal data quality

## The Journey

What started as a side project to follow friends during competitions has grown into a platform serving thousands of orienteers worldwide. Since launch, LiveOL has:

- ✅ Processed **millions of split times**
- ✅ Covered **thousands of competitions**
- ✅ Served users in **50+ countries**
- ✅ Maintained **99.9% uptime** during major events
