# About LiveOL

LiveOL brings real-time orienteering results straight to your phone. Follow competitions as they unfold, track your favorite runners and clubs, compare split times, and never miss a moment from the forest.

The app was created on May 9, 2015, by [Ludvig Larsendahl](/ludvig-larsendahl) during his second year of high school in Sweden. What started as a simple side project has grown over nearly a decade into a trusted companion for the orienteering community, continuously improved and refined along the way.


In 2023, a subscription model was introduced to help cover running costs while keeping the app sustainable for the future. LiveOL is available in multiple languages including Swedish, English, Norwegian, German, Serbian, and Spanish. Made possible by wonderful contributions from orienteers across Europe. Do you want to help translating the app, [click here](/translate).

Based in Sweden but with a vision to serve the global orienteering community. Here's to the next decade of live results 🎉
