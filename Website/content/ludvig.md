# About Ludvig Larsendahl

**Ludvig Larsendahl** is the creator and lead developer of LiveOL, combining his passion for orienteering with software engineering expertise.

## Background

Ludvig is a competitive orienteer and software developer who saw the need for better real-time results tracking in the sport. What started as a personal project to follow friends during competitions has evolved into a comprehensive platform used by the orienteering community worldwide.

## The Vision

LiveOL was built to solve a simple problem: making orienteering competitions more accessible and exciting to follow in real-time. The platform provides:

- **Live tracking** of runners during competitions
- **Detailed analysis** of race performance
- **Historical results** and statistics
- **Editorial content** breaking down technical aspects of races

## Technical Approach

The platform emphasizes:

- **Precision**: Accurate data processing and display
- **Performance**: Fast, responsive interfaces
- **Clarity**: Clean, blueprint-inspired design for easy data interpretation
