# Translate the app

LiveOL is already available in a bunch of different languages, but we'd love to reach even more orienteers around the world.

*A lot of the app is as of now translated using AI, so some things might sound a bit off. Bear with us and please reach out if you spot something that needs fixing!*

If you're fluent in a language we don't support yet, or want to improve an existing translation, we'd love your help. As a thank you, bigger contributions are **rewarded with 1 year of LiveOL+ for free**.

Interested? [Get in touch](/contact) and let us know which language you can help with.
